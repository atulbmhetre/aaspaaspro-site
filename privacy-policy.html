Privacy Policy

App: Aaspaas Pro  ·  Last updated: 21 June 2026

SAMTech ("we", "us", or "our") operates Aaspaas Pro, a hyperlocal service marketplace that connects customers with local vendors across India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services at aaspaaspro.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate Aaspaas Pro and deliver a reliable local marketplace experience. This may include:

  • Phone number — used to create and verify your account, identify you across sessions, and enable communication related to orders.
  • Location data — with your permission, we collect approximate or precise location to show nearby vendors, calculate service areas, and support delivery or help requests.
  • Device information — such as device type, operating system, app version, and a unique device identifier used for security, notifications, and fraud prevention.
  • Order and usage history — including messages, order status, ratings, saved vendors, addresses, appointment times, and in-app preferences.
  • Profile and vendor information — if you register as a vendor, we collect business details such as shop name, category, service mode, photos, and verification-related data.
  • Communications — support messages, feedback, and notifications sent through the app.

We do not require unnecessary personal data to use core features of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To connect customers with local vendors for delivery, appointments, help, and other hyperlocal services.
  • To create, manage, and secure your account and authenticate your identity.
  • To send order updates, reminders, and other service-related notifications (push and in-app).
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience through aggregated analytics.
  • To enforce our terms, prevent abuse, and maintain platform safety and trust.
  • To comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests from authorities.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not rent or trade your information to advertisers or data brokers.

We may share limited information only when necessary:

  • With vendors — when you place an order, relevant details (such as your phone number, delivery address, order message, or location where required) are shared with the vendor fulfilling your request.
  • With service providers — trusted partners who help us operate the app (e.g. cloud hosting, SMS/OTP delivery, push notifications, payment processing), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • For legal reasons — when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and SAMTech.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations.

  • Customer accounts — order history and profile data are kept while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.
  • Vendor accounts — after a vendor deactivates their account, business-related data is retained for up to 30 days for dispute resolution, billing, and operational continuity, after which it is deleted or anonymised unless law requires longer retention.
  • Backups and logs — residual copies in secure backups may persist for a limited period before automatic purging.

Upon a verified deletion request, we will remove or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to exceptions required by law.

5. Children's Privacy

Aaspaas Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

6. OTP Verification & Identity

We use one-time passwords (OTP) sent via SMS to verify your phone number and secure your account. OTP codes are used solely for authentication and are not stored after verification. Your phone number serves as your primary identity on the platform. Do not share OTP codes with anyone, including persons claiming to represent Aaspaas Pro or SAMTech.

7. Payment Information

Certain vendor subscription or payment features are processed by Razorpay, a third-party payment gateway. SAMTech does not store your full card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment credentials on our servers. Payment data is handled directly by Razorpay in accordance with their privacy policy and applicable PCI-DSS standards. We may receive limited transaction metadata (such as payment status, amount, and reference ID) to confirm subscriptions and maintain billing records.

8. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent (e.g. location), you may withdraw consent through device or app settings.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app account deletion option or email us at support@aaspaaspro.com. We will respond within a reasonable period.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we encourage you to keep your device and OTP confidential.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of Aaspaas Pro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Material changes may be communicated through the app or other appropriate channels.
